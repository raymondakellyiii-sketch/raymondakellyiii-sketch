How to Organise Your Cassettes

Organizing your cassettes is very importaint in the proccess of starting a collection!

Cassettte Shelf

One of the best ways of doing this is purchasing a cassette shelf, these shelfs are custom built shelf meant specifically for organizing your collection. The shelf is recomended for the best and safest storage, there is a less chance that they can be damaged from falling this way.

Catigorizing

Catigorizing your cassettes is neccisary for quickly locating a tape you want to listen to. The best way to catigorize your cassettes is to organze them by genre, then by artist name/album title alphabetically by first two letters, this way if you wanted to listen to Bon Jovi's Runnaway you can look at the rock catigory and locate Rock/Bo/Ru.

Small Collections

If you don't exactly care for a big collection, you can always store the cassettes on top of your sterio or in a box. Strict organization is an option not a necesity. It would be recomended that you store them properly as explained on the care page to better maintain the cassettes themselves.
